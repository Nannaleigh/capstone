Welcome to the Dawg Haus

We're more than just a shelter. We are dedicated to rescuing and caring for dogs and strive to give them the best opportunity to find their forever home. At Dawg Haus, we are a nonprofit animal welfare organization. We rely on funding and support from individuals, businesses, and foundations. We welcome any dog that can be legally admitted. We aim to find the best and most humane outcome for the animals in our care and are dedicated to providing each animal in our care a loving home.

About

The Dawg Haus was founded in 1970 by Georgia Peaches, who worked with animals all her life. She spent 20 years as a veterinarian before pursuing her dream and opening her home to stray and homeless dogs everywhere. Now, over 50 years later, the facility houses dogs from all across the country until they can find their perfect fur-ever home!
