Welcome to the Dawg Haus

Preparing for a new furry family member can be a daunting task. Feel free to make use of our to-do list to check off the items and tasks you'll need to do to prepare for your new arrival. We recommend having the basics, such as a food and water bowl, collar, leash, harness, crate, and any grooming supplies you may need. Feel free to also add training to the list and talk to one of our experts about how you can make the most out of your relationship with your hound.
